Terms of Use (Summary)

This summary is not legal advice but helps subscribers understand how they can use the dataset and the site.

  • Code: MIT license — free to use and redistribute.
  • OSM-derived data: ODbL — attribution required and share-alike for redistributed database derivatives.
  • TIGER/Line data: public domain — free to reuse.
  • To prevent redistribution of OSM-derived raw datasets, do not distribute raw data; use server-side renderers and provide tiles or map services instead of data files.
